Session Overview

Date & Time: 6 June 1962, 6:00–8:00 PM

Studio: Studio Two or Three (memories differ)

Producers: George Martin, Ron Richards

Engineers: 1st – Norman Smith; 2nd – Chris Neal

Recorded Songs: Bésame Mucho, Love Me Do, P.S. I Love You, Ask Me Why

Session Notes & Anecdotes

The Beatles arrived in a white van, looking very thin. John Skinner, the security officer, recalled the band's arrival for what may have been an artist test. Memories differ on which studio was used and whether this was an actual session or just a test.

Ron Richards initially took charge of the session, while Norman Smith was installed as the Beatles’ engineer for all future EMI sessions.
